Canteen Rush AI - Hackathon Demo Guide

Step-by-step walkthrough to pitch to judges successfully.

Judges want to see three major points during your demo: The AI prediction accuracy, dynamic load adaptability, and the zero-crowd friction-free checkout. Use this checklist during your live pitch:

1. Staggered Arrival

Place a customized order in the Student App Simulator choosing a target window. Show how the KDS doesn't tell the vendor to start immediately, but schedules preparation systematically in the queue.

2. Simulating a Surge

Click the "Trigger Class End Peak Rush" button in God Mode. Show how the AI prediction adapts to sudden queues and schedules new orders further out automatically.

3. Zero-Queue Pickup

Mark an order as ready in the KDS. Show how the student's phone instantly updates via WebSocket, showing a secure QR code for contactless collection.

Team Pitch Pro-Tip:

Emphasize to the judges: "We did not build a standard food delivery app. We built an intelligent coordination engine that transforms synchronous queuing jams into distributed schedules."
